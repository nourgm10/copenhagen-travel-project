Why Copenhagen?

Copenhagen, the city of Everlasting Hygge

Legend has it that Copenhagen earned its nickname from the warm embrace of its atmosphere, akin to a perpetual hug from a dear friend. Just like a comforting embrace, Copenhagen's story began with its cozy nooks and crannies, where locals sought refuge from the chill winds that sweep through the city. It wasn't until the 21st century, with a nod to its Scandinavian roots and a sprinkle of fairy dust, that Copenhagen affectionately became known as 'hyggens hjem' - the home of hygge. Today, Copenhagen wears its hygge like a badge of honor. Amidst the blend of historic landmarks, charming cafes serving up steaming cups of hot cocoa and delectable Danish pastries, and bustling markets brimming with artisanal crafts, you can still feel the gentle embrace of hygge in every corner. With every turn down cobblestone streets, you'll discover pockets of warmth – from the flickering glow of candlelit windows to the laughter of friends gathered around crackling fires – all enveloping you in Copenhagen's timeless and inviting embrace.

My favorite cafés in Copenhagen

Grød

Grød, quite literally meaning porridge, embodies the Danish love affair with oats. Their menu offers a delightful array of porridge toppings, showcasing an impressive variety that could satisfy even the most discerning palate. Take, for instance, their 'All In' bowl, a veritable cornucopia of flavors featuring apple compote, caramel sauce, peanut butter, Icelandic yoghurt, almonds, fresh fruit, and the list goes on. It's a tantalizing experience that leaves one longing for more.

What I like about it

As someone who has traversed the globe in search of nourishing culinary delights, Grød's innovative approach to porridge is a delightful discovery. Each spoonful is a testament to their commitment to wholesome and flavorful ingredients, making it a destination worth seeking out, no matter where in the world you may find yourself.

Kaf

Kaf, nestled on a serene street in the vibrant heart of Nørrebro, offers a tranquil escape from the bustling city. Whether you choose to bask under the shade of oak trees or cozy up indoors, the ambiance sets the stage for an indulgent experience. Meticulously crafted pastries await, each a testament to the artistry and dedication of the bakers, complemented by a carefully curated drink selection.

What I like about it

As someone who values both flavor and sustainability in my culinary adventures, Kaf's commitment to using seasonal ingredients shines through in every bite. The ever-rotating menu reflects the changing seasons, offering a delightful array of pastries that capture the essence of each time of year. With a selection of cutting-edge cakes, Kaf elevates the vegan dining experience to new heights.

The Artisan

The Artisan, nestled by the picturesque lakes in Copenhagen, promises a coffee experience like no other. Here, innovation meets tradition as we are invited to embark on a journey through the world of specialty coffee. From roasting their own beans to savoring their inventive coffee cocktails, every visit is an opportunity to explore the nuances of coffee. Immerse yourself in the rich aromas of exquisite coffees and discover an innovative take on a traditional menu, as The Artisan has something to satisfy every coffee lover's palate.

What I like about it

At The Artisan, you can have your preferred coffee roasted exactly how you like it. Whether light, medium, or full dark city roast, we'll customize it to your taste. It takes approximately 12 minutes to roast your batch, and in the meantime, you can relax or supervise the process while enjoying one of our special roast profiles.
