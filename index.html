Why Copenhagen?

Copenhagen, the city of Everlasting Hygge

Legend has it that Copenhagen earned its nickname from the warm embrace of its atmosphere, akin to a perpetual hug from a dear friend. Just like a comforting embrace, Copenhagen's story began with its cozy nooks and crannies, where locals sought refuge from the chill winds that sweep through the city. It wasn't until the 21st century, with a nod to its Scandinavian roots and a sprinkle of fairy dust, that Copenhagen affectionately became known as 'hyggens hjem' - the home of hygge. Today, Copenhagen wears its hygge like a badge of honor. Amidst the blend of historic landmarks, charming cafes serving up steaming cups of hot cocoa and delectable Danish pastries, and bustling markets brimming with artisanal crafts, you can still feel the gentle embrace of hygge in every corner. With every turn down cobblestone streets, you'll discover pockets of warmth – from the flickering glow of candlelit windows to the laughter of friends gathered around crackling fires – all enveloping you in Copenhagen's timeless and inviting embrace.
